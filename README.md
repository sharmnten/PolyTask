## PolyTask

</div>

PolyTask is a simple calendar application integrated with a few intelligent features.
### 1. Auto Scheduler
Algorithm that slots tasks into your free time, to maximize time efficiency and reduce your stress.
### 2. Task Categorizer
Automatically color-codes your scheduler and categorizes tasks into specific categories.
